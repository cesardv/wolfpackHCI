Welcome to Woofpack.info, your resource for all you need to know about dogs.

Thanks for visiting! Here you'll find lots of information regarding dog breeds, and their different characteristics and history.

Whether you already have a dog, or are looking to become a future dog-owner, you will find lots of interesting information about many different breeds of Canis lupus familiaris! And there are quite a few canines out there, from the tiny Chihuahuas to the tallest Irish Wolfhounds!
