About Us

Woofpack.info's goal is to provide useful information about several dog breeds, and general information about dogs, their different behaviors, and how to feed and care for one. Our objective is that future dog owners learn more about the different types of dog breeds using our site and thus are able to make informed and educated decisions when choosing a dog to fit their lifestyle. Dog breeds are plenty, and each has its own unique characteristics and they all do better within environments/lifestyles that complement them. Additionally, many dog owners are unaware that some common household substances or food items, though safe for humans, might be very toxic to dogs. Another one of our goals, is to spread the word about some of these potentially dangerous foods and prevent accidents or pet deaths due to the pet-owner's misinformation.

Team Wolfpack was reponsible for this site.

Woofpack.info was created in the Spring of 2012 by a group of undergraduate Georgia State University students for a Human-Computer Interaction class under Dr. Scott Owen.
